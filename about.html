About Coding Contest Radar

Our Mission

Coding Contest Radar aims to provide a comprehensive platform for coders to discover and participate in exciting coding contests from top platforms. We believe in the power of competitive programming to enhance problem-solving skills and provide a platform for learning and growth.

Our Features

  • Discover upcoming coding contests from top platforms
  • Get contest details including start time and duration
  • Direct links to register for contests
  • Countdown timers for each contest
